# BreadBoard Power Supply
When building circuits on the breadboard and you need more power than your MCU can provide, you need something better than your bench power supply which is noisy (large cooling fan) and needs some setup to tune voltage and current.

So we need something that:
- Plugs directly on the breadboard; therefore, no wires exist.
- Have an on/off switch.
- Can provide 5V and 3.3V power.
- Power can be drawn from various wall power supplies, from 6V to 12V.
