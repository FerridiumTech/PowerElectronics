## Cuk Converter
This Cuk converter from **Fundamentals of Power Electronics Robert W. Erickson & Dragan Maskimovic** is a switching Buck-Boost converter that inverts polarity.
It has two-pole low-pass filters. In a well designed converter, in which the capacitor provides significant filtering of the switching ripple , Capacitance **C** is chosen large enough that its impedance at the swithing frequency is much smaller than the load impedance **R**.
Hence nearly all of the inductor current ripple flows through the capacitor, and very little flows through the load.
